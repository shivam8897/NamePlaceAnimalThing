## 🎮 What Is This Game?

**Name Place Animal Thing** is the beloved pen-and-paper word game from your childhood — the one played in classrooms, on road trips, at family dinners — **now live, multiplayer, and gloriously chaotic online.**

One random letter. Four categories. Thirty seconds. Infinite arguments about whether "Zorse" is a real animal.

You know the drill. You've always loved it. **Now play it with anyone, anywhere, instantly.**

## 🚀 How to Play

```
1.  Open the game → nameplaceanimalthing.app
          ↓
2.  Create a room  OR  join with a friend's code
          ↓
3.  Share the room code with your crew 📋
          ↓
4.  A random letter drops for ALL players at once 🎲
          ↓
5.  30 seconds on the clock — TYPE FAST ⏱️
          ↓
6.  Hit  DONE  when your four answers are ready 🔥
          ↓
7.  Answers revealed — duplicates score less, uniques score more 📊
          ↓
8.  5 rounds total → highest score takes the crown 👑
          ↓
9.  Argue, laugh, rematch. Repeat forever. 🔄
```

## 🏅 Scoring System

Points are awarded **per valid answer**, based on uniqueness across all players:

| Situation | Points |
|---|---|
| ✅ Unique valid answer (only you got it) | **10 points** |
| 🤝 Shared valid answer (others got the same) | **5 points** |
| ❌ Blank or invalid answer | **0 points** |
| 🚀 First player to submit all four | **+2 bonus points** |
| 🎯 All four categories filled correctly | **+3 bonus points** |

> 💡 **Pro tip:** "Rhinoceros" and "Rhino" are the same animal — don't waste your uniqueness on obvious answers. Go for "Ratel" or "Rhebok" and watch everyone stare blankly at you. Then win.

## 🧠 Strategy Tips

**⚡ Speed vs. Safety**
Common answers like "Michael" for M-Names are safe but you'll share the points. Rare answers like "Mordecai" win big — if they're valid.

**🌍 Geography is your secret weapon**
Most players exhaust their country knowledge fast. Study your maps. "Montenegro" beats "Mexico" every single time.

**🐻 Animals are a trap**
Everyone picks the obvious one — "Mouse", "Monkey". Go obscure. "Mandrill" beats "Mouse". "Maned Wolf" wins the whole category.

**⚡ Things are wild cards**
Don't overthink it. Any noun works. "Microwave" in 3 seconds beats "Magnetohydrodynamic generator" typed in 31 seconds.

**👑 Names are a minefield**
Fictional characters usually count. "Moana" ✅. "Mufasa" ✅. "My dog Max" ❌. Know the house rules before you play.

**🏃 Submit early, always**
The +2 speed bonus adds up across 5 rounds. It's frequently the tiebreaker between equally-matched players.
