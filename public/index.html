— Rules —

How to Play N·P·A·T

1
Create a Room

Get a 4-letter code and share it with friends. Up to 8 players per room.

2
Get a Letter

A random letter slams onto the screen. The 45-second clock starts ticking.

3
Fill All Four

Type a Name, Place, Animal, and Thing for that letter — before anyone else.

4
Score & Win

Unique answers = 10 pts. Shared = 5 pts. First to submit gets a +5 speed bonus!
